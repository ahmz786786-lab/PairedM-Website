Getting Started Before You Begin

Your data security is important to us. We are GDPR compliant and committed to protecting your privacy. Please read through our guidelines to ensure you have the right engagement at all matching levels.

  • Remain anonymous until you are comfortable sharing your details
  • Avoid including personal contact information in your profile
  • Don't share your telephone number, email, home address, or surname
  • Create a dedicated email account for your PairedM profile
  • Use a unique and confidential password not used elsewhere

Level 1 & 2 Initial Meetings

Our coordinators help you learn about your potential match before sharing any personal contact details. These meetings are conducted face-to-face with coordinator support on every call.

  • Multiple Level 1 and 2 meetings are encouraged before progressing
  • Female members should bring their wali (guardian) to all face-to-face calls
  • Coordinators help ensure appropriate questions are asked
  • We alert users to any inconsistencies identified during meetings
  • Contact info@pairedm.co.uk if you have concerns about a member after a meeting

Level 2 & 3 Getting to Know Each Other

Take your time getting acquainted and proceed at a pace that feels right. For international matches, understanding cultural differences is especially important.

  • Focus on getting to know your potential match gradually
  • Consider cultural differences, especially for international matches
  • Level 3 involves family introductions
  • Personal details are shared at Level 3 — not before
  • Our team continues to provide guidance and support

Level 3+ Beyond & Nikah

Before engagement, take the time to consider all aspects carefully. Understand your rights and be fully informed before making any commitments.

  • Identity validation occurs from Level 3 meetings onward
  • Research legal protections in your partner's country for international matches
  • Understand your rights regarding domestic matters
  • Our ongoing support continues beyond Level 3
  • We can help with wedding planning and Nikah arrangements
